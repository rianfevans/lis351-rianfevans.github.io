Rian Evans is a senior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, graduatiing in the spring of 2023. She is majoring in Communications Arts with a certificate in Digital Studies. She hopes to pursue a career in Digital Marketing and Social Media Content Strategy. In her free time, she plans to work on her photography, videography, and podcast/audio recording. In the past few years at UW-Madison, she has become skilled in editing programs including Adobe Suite, Abelton Live, and Garageband. This website features some of her personal work and links to her social media pages!
